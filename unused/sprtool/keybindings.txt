GENERAL
q: invert BG color

MOVEMENT
<with ctrl: gotta go fast>
<with shift: resize, if applicable>
hjkl/arrow keys: move sprite/selected hitbox(es)
i/o: previous/next sprite

HITBOXES
a: new hurtbox (attack hitbox)
s: new hitbox (defense hitbox)
p/n: select previous/next hitbox
d: deselect
x: delete selected

SELECTING
ctrl+a: select/deselect all


COPY/PASTE
ctrl+c (all hitboxes selected): copy hitboxes
ctrl+v (after previous): paste hitboxes (OVERWRITES EXISTING)
ctrl+c (no hitboxes selected): copy position
ctrl+v (after previous): paste position
